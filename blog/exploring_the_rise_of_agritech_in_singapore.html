Expert Articles on Exploring the Rise of Agritech in Singapore

Agritech, or agricultural technology, is gaining traction in Singapore as the nation seeks innovative solutions to improve food security and sustainability. Vertical farming, hydroponics, and smart farming technologies are helping Singapore overcome its land constraints to boost local food production.

The government is supporting agritech startups and investing in research to create a sustainable food ecosystem. These initiatives are critical to achieving Singapore's goal of producing 30% of its nutritional needs locally by 2030.

As agritech continues to develop, Singapore is leading the way in sustainable and efficient farming practices that could serve as a model for other urbanized nations.
